Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)
1. What is Online Banking?
Online Banking is a tool that allows you to use a personal computer with an Internet connection to conduct your banking online.
2. What is special about Internet banking?
Internet banking is the most convenient way to bank- anytime, any place, at your convenience.
3. I do not have a PC?
You can access Online from any computer that has connectivity to the Internet. But make sure your computer is Malware free.
4.Is any of my personal information stored in the Online Banking product?
Yes, the Online Banking product stores your User ID, password and user preferences.
5.Can I view my account details in more than one way?
Yes, you can view your accounts by date, check number, transaction, description, or amount in ascending or descending order..
